About this project

We were given a challenge from Bigtincan, and came up with a design solution.

Our Challenge from Bigtincan

Design a solution to provide a better environment for sellers to interact with B2B buyers.

Discover

First we did desk research and conducted user interviews to define our problem.


What is B2B & Our goal?


B2B is short for business-to-business, and refers to the commerce that happens between more than two business/companies. After COVID, lots of B2B business process transitioned to online and virtual. Our group aimed to solve a better interaction method for them.
