About this project

We were given a challenge from Bigtincan, and came up with a design solution.

Our Challenge from Bigtincan

Design a solution to provide a better environment for sellers to interact with B2B buyers.

Discover

First we did desk research and conducted user interviews to define our problem.

What is B2B & Our goal?

B2B is short for business-to-business, and refers to the commerce that happens between more than two business/companies. After COVID, lots of B2B business process transitioned to online and virtual. Our group aimed to solve a better interaction method for them.

Understanding B2B Journey
User Journey

From the journey we found out two things: First, the buyer's journey is complete before the prospect reaches out to a salesperson. Second, sellers and buyers have different preferences for communicating and meeting during the selling process.These differences make B2B seller frustrated on the first stage of the B2B sales process.

From this analysis our insight was:

Problem Statement

How can we improve the way of interacting between sellers and buyers during the first stage of the selling process?

Insights

Our key insights are that buyers reach out to sellers mostly through seller's website, buyers care about the solution, how the solution it will help them to solve their problem and how privacy places a big role in trusting their sellers.

Solution Idea

Our solution idea is to give buyers personalized solutions, so that they can have better understanding of the product they're going to buy

Where we found our solution
Ideation for Solution

Design

Ai.Sell is a tool that can help B2B seller by creating personalized videos for B2B buyers who visit seller's website. It's a plugin for website that uses A.I. technology to create contents.
