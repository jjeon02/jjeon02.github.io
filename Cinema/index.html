LATE NIGHT NOSTALGIA TRIP

Share Your Nostalgia

Late Night Nostalgia Trip is a (virtual) school project funded by your favorite streaming services, and provide you a chance to get along with people while binge-watching nostalgic films and tv shows.


Our binge-watching meetings on every Saturday, GD commons from 6:00 PM to next day 1:00AM. On every meeting, we will watch nostalgic movies and tv shows suggested by participants through this website. Snacks will be provided while watching and socializing together.

Thank you for your suggestion!
