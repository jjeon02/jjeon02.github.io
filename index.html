About me

Hello there! My name is Jueun Jeon (전주은) and I am a UI/UX designer from South Korea currently based in NYC. I went to RISD for Graphic Design and currently pursuing an MFA at the School of Visual Arts for Interaction Design (STEM Designated). I recently worked for PNY Technologies as a web design intern.

My biggest passion is UX Design, which led me to experiences in web/app design. In addition to UX experience, I have done digital content creation and illustration.

In my free time I code my own website, paint and play bass guitar. I am currently looking for more UX opportunities, and would love to get connected!
