About me

Hello there! My name is Jueun Jeon (전주은) and I am a UI/UX designer from South Korea. I went to RISD for BFA Graphic Design and graduated from the School of Visual Arts for MFA Interaction Design. I recently worked for Keykycode as a UX/UI Designer.

My biggest passion is User Experience Design, which led me to experiences in web/app design. In addition to User Experience, I have done User Interface, digital content creation and illustration.

In my free time I code my own website, paint and play bass guitar. I am currently looking for more User Experience Design opportunities, and would love to get connected!
